About

Hello! My name is Ethan Graupmann. I'm a software and game developer based in Colorado. I have a bunch of personal projects ranging from big RPG games to smaller mods for games I enjoy. I also continue to develop my skills in Python, Java, C# and C++, and front-end development.

When I'm not focused on my passions in coding and development, I'm often spending time with my family in Colorado, or working as a Lifeguard in-state.

My passion comes from telling stories and achievement. Software Development is a process- And in that process, I find a satisfaction in learning and growing- Moreover, the sensation of completing a project or feature is one of great satisfaction for me! Game Development and design is another passion of mine- I see game design as the art of telling stories and creating art in a medium that allows the audience to interact directly with it, and RPGs in particular give the audience a new perspective, through the eyes of the characters.

Of Acid and Steel

My largest (And still undergoing) personal project, this is a Turn-Based, Fantasy RPG created using RPG Maker. Although the game itself is created mainly using the events in the engine, I've had to develop several script-based events and use a few add-ons as well! The coding is done in JavaScript, and all of the work in this game is my own- Art, Character development, Combat, and More!

I have a personal goal to make this game all on my own, no matter how long it takes- And I plan on getting into every part of it, from music to animation. During the fist few months of working on the beginning, I've gotten over 50 downloads in it!
